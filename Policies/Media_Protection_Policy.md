MEDIA PROTECTION POLICY  
Version: 1.0   
Approval Date: 7th July, 2026   
Owner: Information Security Officer (ISO)   
Classification: Internal / Restricted
________________________________________
1. Purpose  
The purpose of this Media Protection Policy is to establish requirements for safeguarding digital and physical media containing Veltrix Pay information, including cardholder and customer financial data, throughout its lifecycle, including creation, storage, transport, use, and destruction.  
________________________________________
2. Scope  
This policy applies to:  
•	All employees, contractors, and third parties  
•	All Veltrix Pay information systems  
•	All forms of media, including:  
o	Hard drives, SSDs  
o	USB drives  
o	Backup tapes  
o	CDs/DVDs  
o	Printed documents (statements, KYC documentation)  
o	Mobile devices storing organizational data  
________________________________________
3. Policy Statements  
3.1 Media Access Control  
•	Access to media containing sensitive data must be restricted to authorized personnel only.  
•	Media must be stored in secure, access-controlled areas.  
3.2 Media Labeling  
•	All media containing sensitive information must be labeled according to the organization's data classification standards.  
•	Media containing cardholder data must be labeled "PCI-Restricted."  
3.3 Media Storage  
•	Sensitive media must be stored in locked cabinets, safes, or secured electronic storage systems.  
•	Electronic media must be encrypted when storing confidential information.  
3.4 Media Transport  
•	Transport of media must follow secure handling procedures.  
•	Sensitive media must be encrypted or physically protected during transport.  
•	A chain-of-custody log must be maintained for high-risk media transfers, including KYC document transfers to compliance partners.  
3.5 Media Sanitization & Destruction  
•	Media must be sanitized before reuse or disposal using approved methods such as:  
o	Cryptographic erase  
o	DoD-approved wiping  
o	Physical destruction  
•	Printed documents containing customer financial information must be shredded using cross-cut shredders.  
3.6 Media Retention    
•	Media must be retained only for the period defined by the organization's data retention schedule.  
•	KYC and transaction-related media must be retained for a minimum of 5 years per CBN AML/KYC requirements.  
•	Expired media must be securely destroyed.  
3.7 Prohibited Activities  
•	Use of unapproved USB drives or portable storage devices is prohibited.  
•	Personal devices must not store organizational data unless explicitly authorized.  
________________________________________
4. Roles and Responsibilities  
Information Security Officer (ISO)  
•	Maintain and enforce this policy.  
•	Approve sanitization and destruction procedures.  
IT Department  
•	Implement media protection controls.  
•	Maintain chain-of-custody logs.  
•	Ensure secure storage locations.  
Employees and Contractors  
•	Follow all media protection procedures.  
•	Report lost or stolen media immediately.  
________________________________________
5. Compliance  
Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary actions, up to and including termination, and may expose Veltrix Pay to legal or regulatory penalties.  
________________________________________
6. Review Cycle    
This policy must be reviewed annually or whenever significant changes occur to applicable systems, regulations, or technologies.
