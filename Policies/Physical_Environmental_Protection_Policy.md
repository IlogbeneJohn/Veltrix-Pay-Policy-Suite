PHYSICAL AND ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION POLICY  
Version: 1.0  
Approval Date: 7th July, 2026   
Owner: Information Security Officer (ISO)   
Classification: Internal / Restricted  
________________________________________
1. Purpose  
The purpose of the Physical and Environmental Protection Policy is to define requirements for protecting Veltrix Pay facilities, equipment, and information from physical and environmental threats such as unauthorized access, natural disasters, fire, flooding, and power disruptions.  
________________________________________
2. Scope  
This policy applies to:  
•	All facilities housing Veltrix Pay systems  
•	Employees, contractors, visitors, and third parties  
•	Data centers, server rooms, wiring closets, and workspaces  
•	All organizational hardware and IT infrastructure  
________________________________________
3. Policy Statements  
3.1 Physical Access Control  
•	Facilities must use access controls such as ID badges, key cards, PIN codes, or biometric systems.  
•	Access to sensitive areas (e.g., data centers, the network operations center) must be restricted to authorized personnel only.  
•	Visitor access must be logged and escorted at all times.  
3.2 Facility Monitoring  
•	Security cameras must monitor entrances, exits, and sensitive areas.  
•	Video recordings must be retained according to retention requirements.  
•	Intrusion detection systems must be installed where appropriate.  
3.3 Environmental Controls  
•	Critical equipment must be protected with:  
o	Temperature and humidity monitoring  
o	HVAC systems  
o	Fire detection and suppression systems  
o	Leak detection sensors  
•	Environmental alerts must notify appropriate personnel.  
3.4 Power Protection  
•	Critical systems must be connected to:  
o	Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)  
o	Backup generators where required  
•	Power infrastructure must be regularly tested and maintained, given Veltrix Pay's reliance on consistent uptime for real-time payment processing.  
3.5 Equipment Protection  
•	Equipment must be housed in secure racks or locked rooms.  
•	Portable devices must be locked away when not in use.  
•	Equipment must be protected against damage, theft, and tampering.  
3.6 Physical Asset Management  
•	All hardware assets must be inventoried and tracked.  
•	Asset tags must be used on servers, workstations, and network devices.  
•	Asset changes must follow formal change management processes.  
3.7 Emergency Preparedness  
•	Facilities must maintain emergency evacuation procedures.  
•	Employees must receive training on emergency procedures.  
•	Emergency exits must remain unobstructed at all times.  
________________________________________
4. Roles and Responsibilities  
Information Security Officer (ISO)  
•	Oversees physical security and environmental control compliance.    
Facilities Department  
•	Manages physical building security systems and environmental controls.  
IT Department  
•	Protects IT infrastructure and coordinates equipment security.  
Employees & Contractors  
•	Follow all physical security procedures.  
•	Report incidents or suspicious activity immediately.  
________________________________________
5. Compliance  
Non-compliance may result in disciplinary actions and may expose Veltrix Pay to severe operational or regulatory risk.  
________________________________________
6. Review Cycle
This policy must be reviewed annually or after major facility, security, or technology changes.
